How it works

Step 1 — Upload up to 4 CVs (.tex), give each a title (e.g. Data Analyst, QA, Software Engineer, AI/ML Engineer). Save once — no need to re-upload.
Step 2 — Pick which CV to use, paste a job description, click Optimize.
Step 3 — Click Open in Overleaf — your optimized CV opens directly. Hit Recompile and download PDF.
Note — Your CV is never faked. AI only rephrases what already exists, and keeps it to 1 page.
